JAY VERNON

Born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Jay Vernon was never sure which creative path he would take. He had always been interested in Graphic Design but never really experimented with it until his final year of high school. After studying Graphic Design for two years, he decided to attend the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College. There's no telling where Jay will end up as he has interest in many other fields, but he is prepared to work anywhere in the world. However, if he were to work in the Interactive Media field, Jay has shown great interest in 3D Modeling and video editing.
